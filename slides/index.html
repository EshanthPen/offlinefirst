How the sync spreads
One teacher publishes.
Every nearby device catches up in seconds.
Heartbeat returns peers. New devices auto-connect. When they connect, they swap peer lists. A lesson posted on one tablet propagates to every other tablet on the local WiFi within 30 seconds.
