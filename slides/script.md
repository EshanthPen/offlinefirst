## Delivery notes

- **Pace:** ~150 words per minute. The full script is ~260 words. Practice until you can hit 1:55 without rushing.
- **Tone:** Even, confident, slightly informal. Don't sound like a commercial.
- **Pauses:** One small pause after "two-point-six billion" on slide 1. One small pause after "the whole classroom catches up" on slide 3. Everywhere else, keep moving.
- **Cuts:** Slides 1–3 and 6–8 are clean cuts. Slides 4 and 5 are over-the-shoulder of the actual app — the audio is continuous, only the video source changes.
- **No music** under voiceover. Optional low-volume bed (no lyrics, no drops) only during the app demo if the room is silent.

---

## What NOT to say

- Don't say "AI," "machine learning," or "blockchain." None of those are in the product.
- Don't say "revolutionary," "game-changing," or "disruptive." Judges have heard it.
- Don't read the slide text out loud. The slide and the voice should reinforce each other, not duplicate.
- Don't apologize for limitations. If something doesn't work in the demo, edit it out.
